About

Romeo Adams

Kuils River, Cape Town

Romeo Adams Consulting Services is an owner-managed consultancy, providing predominantly human resource management and development consulting, but also providing project management and parliamentary practice and procedure consulting. The consultancy establishes relationships with previously disadvantaged communities and stakeholders who want to contribute towards the development of such communities.

Romeo Adams is a seasoned executive leader with over two decades of strategic management experience across South Africa’s public sector. His career spans high-impact roles equivalent to Director-General and Chief Executive Officer, including his most recent tenure as Secretary to the Western Cape Provincial Parliament (2019–2025). Known for his exceptional analytical acumen, emotional intelligence, and visionary leadership, Romeo consistently aligns institutional goals with transformative action.

He holds a Master’s Degree in the discipline of Industrial Psychology and has led diverse portfolios in justice, correctional services, tourism, and legislative governance. Romeo’s core strengths include strategic planning, financial oversight, human capital management and development, and stakeholder engagement at the highest levels—ranging from parliamentary authorities to international organisations.

Romeo is a published thought leader and international speaker, contributing to global dialogues on legislative modernisation, cybersecurity, and executive accountability. His governance expertise is further reflected in board memberships across education, sport, and public service institutions. A registered Master HR Professional with the South African Board of Peoples Practice, Romeo blends technical mastery with a deep commitment to service delivery innovation and institutional excellence.

Outside of his professional realm, Romeo is a passionate sports administrator and coach, having led rugby and baseball federations with the same rigor and pride he brings to public service. His leadership is defined by integrity, resilience, and a relentless pursuit of progress.
